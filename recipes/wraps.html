CHICKEN SALAD WRAPS

Description

culinary dish made with a soft flatbread rolled around a filling.

ingredients

  • romaine lettuce
  • tomatoes &ripe avocado
  • tortilla wraps

Steps

  1. combine all salad ingredients together
  2. Pour dressing over salad
  3. Add salad to a tortilla wrap or large lettuce leave, over greens or dip style with crackers or chips.
